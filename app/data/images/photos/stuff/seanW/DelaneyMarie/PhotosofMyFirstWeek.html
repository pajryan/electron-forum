It has been a good week.  All things considered, of course.  With the breathing air for the first time and the bright lights and all.  You are probably wondering why I took a little while in getting here last Wednesday morning.  Sorry if I made you worry, but I wasn’t quite ready and I really don’t appreciate feeling rushed.  
 
My Mom and Dad are pretty nice, I think.  I gotta admit, I was a little nervous about meeting them.  They don’t look like aliens or anything.
 
I don’t mean to brag, but in case you were wondering, I am a pretty darn good baby.   I rarely fuss and love to sleep for three to four hours straight in the early morning.
