Photos of My Second Week
 
Well, I survived my second week here on earth.  What do I like best about being here?  When my Mom and Dad hold me tight.  What do I like the least?  Dirty diapers.  It’s been a busy week.  My Nana, Papa brought my Aunt Yvette in from Colorado for a visit.  It’s nice to meet more of the family.  My BFF Zoe came over for a visit - she brought her parents, Emmett and Kelley.  My other BFF Eliza came over with her parents, Adam and Amanda.  I don’t mean to brag, but everyone said how beautiful I am and what a good baby I am.  It’s nice to be admired.  My Aunt Laura and Uncle Bart also came to visit.  I met Laura at my birth, but I was a little traumatized at the time - it was good to see her when I was feeling and looking better.
 
It’s been an exciting week.  I tried out my gymini and my tummy time surf board, I went with Mom and Meme to Mom’s midwife and I went to see my doctor - Dr. Landman.  After two weeks, I now weigh 7 pounds and 6 ounces - I regained my birth weight and then some (I’m a really good eater).  Dr. Landman said I was “thriving!”  I also took my first sponge bath...I’m undecided about this bathing thing.  Mom says I’ll like to soak in a tub when I’m older, but I’m not too sure.
 
I’ve found my voice this week which I use to let my parents know what I need.  It probably sounds like I’m high maintenance, but really, all I need is either food, a diaper change or love.  Check out the photos of my second week below - I think you’ll agree that it was a good one!
