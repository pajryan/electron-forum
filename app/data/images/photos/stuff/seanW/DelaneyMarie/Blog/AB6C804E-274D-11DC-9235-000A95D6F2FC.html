My First Trip to Texas
 
My first trip to Texas was great!  I love flying, which is a terrific opportunity to catch up on sleep.  My Mom, Meme, and I flew out on Tuesday and my Dad flew in on Thursday night with my friend Zoe and her parents, Uncle E and Aunt Kelley.  My Uncle Bart and Aunt La also came in late on Thursday night.  
 
On Friday morning, after Dad and Uncle E successfully finished their battle with the car seats, we drove out to Cedar Creek Lake for some water skiing and fun in the sun.  It was a lot hotter than home, but I had a lot of fun.  
 
We were supposed to return on Monday, but big storms kept us in Dallas until Tuesday.  It was a good trip, but my parents kept saying things like, “Man, vacation sure is less vacation-like with a kid.”  You wouldn’t think it, but itty bitty me and little Zoe seemed to keep all four of our parents pretty busy the entire time.  Four adults, two kids, for four days . . . and about 12 suitcases, strollers, car seats, and diaper bags.
