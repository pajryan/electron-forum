Bay Cruise for MD’s Birthday
 
I can’t believe I don’t have a picture of me with MD on his birthday cruise around the bay!  It was a really beautiful morning on the boat and I loved being outside with the seagulls flying all around us.  
 
My Aunt Yvette came in from Colorado, and Annie came down from Seattle.  We also celebrated Annie’s birthday, Assad’s birthday, and my parents’ anniversary.   Lots to celebrate.
