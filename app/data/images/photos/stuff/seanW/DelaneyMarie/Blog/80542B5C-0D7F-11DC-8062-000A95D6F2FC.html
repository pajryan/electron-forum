First Swim
 
This Memorial Day weekend I went over to Nana and Papa’s with my Mom, Dad, Aunt Mellie, Mike and Courtney for some fun in the sun.  We played games, ate great food (milk is really wonderful in new places) and, best of all, went for a swim.  I borrowed my friend Zoe’s bathing suit.  It was a little bit big, but it did the trick.
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
The water was really warm and I loved it!  My Dad dunked me under the water a few times and I held my breath.  
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
My Mom took me all around the pool and I kicked my little legs as fast as I could.  I can’t wait to swim again!
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
Saturday, May 26, 2007
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
After my swim I was all tuckered out.  My Nana warmed me right up and I took a nap outside next to the pool.  What a great day!
