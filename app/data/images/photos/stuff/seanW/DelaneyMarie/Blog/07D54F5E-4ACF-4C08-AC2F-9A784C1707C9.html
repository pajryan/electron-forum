Reduce, Reuse, Recycle
 
My Mom and I went to see some of my old buddies (we were all born about the same time, and it is so nice to be around people who just seem to understand what you are going through).  
 
Many of my friends were excited to show off all of their new toys and games.  I, on the other hand, was more than happy to bring along and share my Mom’s collapsable cloth shopping bag from Whole Foods.  You know, they not only help out the environment, but they are really fun to chew on.  Of course, I pretty much think most everything is fun to chew on.  But, when given the option, I’ll take a sack or a cup (or pretty much whatever junk you may have around) over a toy any day of the week.
