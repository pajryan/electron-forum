My Aunt Mellie Visits!
 
 
My Aunt Mellie and Mike and my new friend Courtney came out from Dallas just to see me this weekend.  I’m crying in the picture I took with them, but only because I am so excited to see them.  Sometimes, I act like a baby, you know?  We had a fun weekend and Courtney was a big help to my Mom and Dad.  On  Saturday, we went swimming down at Nana and Papa’s house and on Monday morning, we had breakfast together before they all had to catch their flight back to Dallas.  
 
Courtney couldn’t
 
believe that she was ever as small as me.  But she did ask to take this picture of the two of us together so that she could see how much bigger she was than me.  Yep, she’s bigger.  But I have seven years to catch up!
