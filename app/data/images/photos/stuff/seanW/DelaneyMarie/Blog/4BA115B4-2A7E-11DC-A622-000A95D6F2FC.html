First Giants Game
 
Yep, that’s me, watching the Giants shut out the Diamondbacks on a sunny afternoon at AT&T Park.  I made it through the whole game, although I did sleep through the first two and needed a quick change in the top of the eighth.  Barry Bonds played for me (which, after a night game, he usually doesn’t).  No homerun from Barry, but he did hit a double, and I saw homeruns from Klesko and Molina.  Plus, rookie pitcher Tim Lincecum struck out 12!
 
I loved being at the ballpark.  The Giants even gave me a certificate to commemorate my first baseball game.  My Dad almost caught a foul ball, but it was snatched by a guy with a glove two seats away.  
 
Now, I just need to convince the Giants’ management that they finally scored some runs because of me . . .
